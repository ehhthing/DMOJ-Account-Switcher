Instructions

Adding accounts: When adding your first account, simply click "Add User" while signed in on dmoj. When adding more accounts, you must click "Add User". You will then be redirected to the sign in page, and you can sign in. After, click "Add User" again to add your new account.

Updaing accounts: If the stored cookied ever becomes invalidated, you can update the account by right clicking the account while signed in. If multiple cookies become invalid, click "Add Account" and sign into your new account. After, you can right click the account.

Why so complicated?
This extension works by storing cookies of sessions, and restoring them when switching accounts. When you log out, this token is invalidated. To prevent this, clicking "Add Account" will remove the session cookie instead of invalidating it. This keeps previously stored cookies valid.
